Discoveries - Interview Edit

The theme ‘Discovery’ is linked to the current pandemic

We interviewed 4 people of various ages and occupations in Ireland and asked what are their discoveries when confined to home.

Video Context


Discovery is intuitive to human. The pandemic has brought about sudden disruptions to our daily routine of knowing, confining us within the 5k limitation around our home. Stuck with the common sight and occurrence which repeat themselves daily, we begin a discovery process that is made more subtle. They are the walking routes that we would not have stepped on, the part of the sea where we would have never set foot, or the seemingly unexceptional abandoned buildings that we would not have stopped to watch.

What’s more, the pandemic is a chance for self-discovery. Often times we find it hard to know our authentic self, but the lockdown has forced us to spend more quality time with ourselves, which makes us rethink about our personalities, characteristics and life goals.

Tone of Piece


The tone of the piece is formal, impassioned, informative and inspirational, as the answer we got from the interviewees were all very genuine. It is an impassioned piece that inspires people to discover the beauty in the little things that surround us.

As the interviewees all talks about their experiences in Ireland, I find it pertinent to link the discovery to Ireland. This also helps to establish empathy amongst the audience, which I will discuss in the next section.

Intend Effect on Audience


Echoing with the tone, the piece intends to inspire the audience to discover and appreciate the beauty that surrounds us, as well as present to them the charming scenery of Ireland.

More significantly, it intends to encourage the audience, wherever they might be, to appreciate the quotidian mundanity and to achieve self-discovery. Beauty is everywhere. It is not that it is lacking to our eyes, but our eyes which fail to perceive it.
